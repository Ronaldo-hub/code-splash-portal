About the Khoisan First Nations

The Khoisan are the indigenous people of Southern Africa with a history dating back approximately 140,000 years. Their cultural heritage, languages, and traditional knowledge represent some of humanity's oldest living traditions.

The Khoisan Voice initiative advocates for:

  • Land Sovereignty - Recognition of ancestral territories and resource rights
  • Cultural Recognition - Preservation of languages and cultural heritage
  • Political Representation - Direct representation in governance
  • Financial Reparation - Addressing historical injustices
